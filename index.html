Augmented Reality for a Transforming Nation

About the Project

This graduation project explores how Augmented Reality (AR) can accelerate Saudi Arabia’s digital transformation under Vision 2030. The project focuses on creating immersive, interactive experiences that enhance learning, tourism, and smart city planning — all while aligning with the Kingdom's goals for a knowledge-based economy.

Saudi Vision 2030

Saudi Vision 2030 focuses on innovation, tourism, education, and smart infrastructure. Augmented Reality supports these goals by:

  • Tourism: AR can bring ancient sites like AlUla or Diriyah to life with interactive reconstructions and guided virtual tours.
  • Education: AR makes learning more visual and immersive, enhancing STEM and medical training through 3D visualization.
  • Smart Cities: Projects like NEOM and The Line aim to be tech-driven cities — AR can assist with city planning, navigation, and digital interaction.
  • Culture: AR can preserve and promote Saudi heritage through museums and cultural exhibits.
